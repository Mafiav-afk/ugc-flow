# Category Physics

- Skincare/liquids: preserve viscosity, surface tension, absorption time, droplet gravity, and residue; never float, clip, or change volume between cuts.
- Food: preserve steam direction, crumb fall, stretch elasticity, pour volume, bite marks, and utensil contact.
- Cleaning: dirt must move from contact, cloth compression must match pressure, liquid pickup must reduce visible volume.
- Fabric/fashion: preserve drape, wrinkle memory, stretch direction, body contact, seams, and garment continuity.
- Pet: preserve anatomy, paw contact, fur collision, gaze, weight shifts, and non-distressed natural behavior.
- Appliances: preserve buttons, ports, displays, hinges, cable routing, rotating parts, and cause/effect timing.
- Automotive: film while stationary; preserve vehicle interior geometry, mount contact, cable position, reflections, and road-safety constraints.
- Packaging: lock cap, pump, label, color, size, fill line, logo placement, and hand scale from a clean reference.
