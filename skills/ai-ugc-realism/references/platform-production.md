# Platform Production

- Douyin/TikTok/Reels: 9:16, first meaningful motion in 0.3 seconds, hook complete by 2 seconds, safe text inside center 80% width.
- Xiaohongshu: allow slightly slower proof and more specific experience language; avoid hard-sell voice.
- Kuaishou: prioritize direct demonstration, price/value clarity, and conversational continuity.
- WeChat Channels: keep dialogue legible without trend context and make trust/proof explicit.
- Use 30 fps unless fast physical demonstration needs 60 fps source footage.
- Record clean dialogue and product sounds separately when possible. Add music in post.
- Export with stable identity, readable packaging, no captions baked by the generation model, and no platform UI under important visuals.
