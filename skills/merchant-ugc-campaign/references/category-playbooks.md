# Category Playbooks

Use only the matching section.

## Beauty and skincare

- Triggers: texture, finish, routine friction, portability, shade/fit, visible application.
- Hooks: close-up complaint, half-face comparison, bag discovery, partner reaction.
- Hero proof: macro texture, one-swipe application, timed absorption, daylight finish.
- Risks: medical treatment, guaranteed whitening/acne removal, invented SPF or ingredient claims.

## Food and beverage

- Triggers: taste curiosity, preparation speed, portion value, texture, occasion.
- Hooks: first-bite reaction, pantry discovery, blind taste, late-night craving.
- Hero proof: steam, crunch, stretch, pour, preparation timer, portion count.
- Risks: disease prevention, weight-loss guarantees, fabricated origin or organic certification.

## Home and cleaning

- Triggers: visible mess, saved effort, storage, before/after, awkward spaces.
- Hooks: impossible stain, one annoying corner, partner disbelief, “why didn’t I know”.
- Hero proof: one-pass clean, measured water/dust pickup, side-by-side surface.
- Risks: sterilization percentages, material compatibility, exaggerated durability.

## Maternal and baby

- Triggers: caregiver relief, safe handling, night use, portability, cleanup.
- Hooks: real parent pain at a specific time, one-hand operation, bag packing.
- Hero proof: assembly, leak prevention, cleaning steps, size and material labels.
- Risks: absolute safety, medical/development claims, invented age suitability.

## Pet

- Triggers: pet reaction, cleanup, enrichment, feeding friction, odor control.
- Hooks: pet chooses between two objects, owner confession, mess reveal.
- Hero proof: real pet interaction, material/size, cleaning, measured portion.
- Risks: veterinary treatment, guaranteed behavior change, staged distress.

## Electronics and appliances

- Triggers: visual core loop, speed, battery, compatibility, compactness.
- Hooks: raw demo first, screen/measurement proof, old-vs-new friction.
- Hero proof: screen recording, stopwatch, decibel/distance/charge test using supplied data.
- Risks: fabricated benchmarks, universal compatibility, fake app UI.

## Fashion, shoes, and bags

- Triggers: fit, styling range, capacity, fabric behavior, movement comfort.
- Hooks: three-body/three-look switch, “looks small but”, outfit rescue.
- Hero proof: try-on movement, pocket/capacity test, seam and fabric macro.
- Risks: copied designer marks, fake material composition, guaranteed fit.

## Sports and outdoor

- Triggers: setup speed, pack size, grip, stability, weather use, recovery routine.
- Hooks: field failure, backpack reveal, timed setup, beginner mistake.
- Hero proof: real movement, load/stability demo, packed vs deployed dimensions.
- Risks: injury prevention, performance guarantees, unsafe demonstrations.

## Personal care and wellness

- Triggers: daily friction, comfort, hygiene routine, portability, sensory experience.
- Hooks: awkward real-life moment, routine confession, partner/friend recommendation.
- Hero proof: safe visible use, material, noise, cleaning, timer.
- Risks: diagnosis, cure, body transformation, medical device implications.

## Automotive

- Triggers: cabin mess, storage, installation, visibility, charging, emergency readiness.
- Hooks: dashboard/cup-holder problem, passenger reaction, night scenario.
- Hero proof: installation sequence, fit, cable routing, real driving-safe stationary demo.
- Risks: unsafe filming while driving, universal vehicle fit, safety guarantees.

## Local services

- Triggers: location specificity, waiting time, transparent process, atmosphere, result.
- Hooks: neighborhood discovery, price-board reveal, booking friction solved.
- Hero proof: arrival-to-service sequence, real environment, menu/process, verified offer.
- Risks: fake scarcity, undisclosed conditions, fabricated reviews.

## Apps and knowledge products

- Triggers: visual core loop, saved steps, personalized output, notification, progress.
- Hooks: screen result first, notification punchline, old workflow frustration.
- Hero proof: uncut screen recording with real product UI and one complete task.
- Risks: fake interface, income/score guarantees, fabricated user counts.
