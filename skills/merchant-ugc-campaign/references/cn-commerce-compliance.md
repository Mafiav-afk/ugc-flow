# Chinese Commerce Copy Risk Check

This is a practical copy review, not legal advice.

## Flag before publishing

- Absolutes: 最、第一、唯一、顶级、100%、永久、零风险、绝对、万能.
- Medical implications without authorization: 治疗、治愈、消炎、抗菌率、修复疾病、药到病除.
- Guaranteed outcomes: 必瘦、必白、根治、不反弹、立刻见效、人人适用.
- Fabricated authority: 国家级、专家推荐、官方认证、专利配方 unless documented.
- Fabricated social proof: sales, rankings, reviews, view counts, awards, user counts.
- Unclear price/offer conditions: lowest price, limited quantity, gifts, subscriptions, renewal.
- Unsupported comparisons or denigration of competitors.
- Minors, pregnancy, medical, finance, and food-function claims requiring heightened review.

## Safer transformations

- Replace “完全不油” with a visible finish demo plus “我上手感觉更清爽”.
- Replace “一天都有效” with a verified duration or “我的通勤时段里…”.
- Replace “所有车型适用” with the documented compatibility range.
- Replace “三天见效” with an honest use diary and conditions.

## Required output

Return three lists: `safe_claims`, `needs_verification`, and `do_not_use`. Add the exact evidence needed to upgrade each `needs_verification` item.
